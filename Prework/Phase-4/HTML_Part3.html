Witches' Brew

This drink will give you a magical boost on those cold winter nights. While the cauldron can be substituted with a regular pot, you may find the brew loses some of its potency. For a little extra zip, add a few drops of full moon water just before serving.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon of Bone Ash
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Fairy Dust
  • 1/4 cup of Goblin Blood
  • 2 drops of Snake Venom

Instructions:

  1. Place bone ash in the cauldron.
  2. Mix in the fairy dust.
  3. Measure out the goblin blood separately.
  4. Add the snake venom into measured out goblin dreel.
  5. Stir the goblin blood and snake venom well.
  6. Pour the liquid mixture into the cauldron.
